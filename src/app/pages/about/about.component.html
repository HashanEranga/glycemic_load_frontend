About Us

Welcome to Predict GL, a tool for understanding and managing Glycemic Load (GL) in your diet.

This web app was developed as part of a final-year undergraduate research project conducted by the Department of Food Science and Technology, Faculty of Agriculture, University of Peradeniya. Our mission is to empower individuals, health enthusiasts, and researchers with reliable, easy-to-use tools for making informed dietary choices.
